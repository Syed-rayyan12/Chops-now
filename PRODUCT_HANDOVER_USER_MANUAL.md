## 3. Role Overview

### User (Customer)
The user browses restaurants, places orders, and manages their own account.

Main responsibilities:
- Find restaurants and meals
- Add items to cart
- Checkout and make payment
- View order history
- Update personal details

### Restaurant Partner
The restaurant receives orders and prepares food for delivery.

Main responsibilities:
- Keep restaurant details up to date
- Manage menu items and pricing
- Accept or reject incoming orders
- Mark orders ready for rider pickup
- View earnings

### Rider
The rider accepts delivery tasks and completes deliveries.

Main responsibilities:
- Set availability (online/offline)
- Accept delivery requests
- Navigate to customer address
- Mark deliveries completed
- Track earnings

### Admin
The admin oversees the whole platform.

Main responsibilities:
- Monitor orders and performance
- Manage users, restaurants, and riders
- Review analytics and reports
- Handle operational issues and support

---

## 4. How the Platform Works End-to-End
The platform works as one connected flow:

1. User places an order
- User chooses a restaurant, adds items, and checks out.

2. Restaurant processes the order
- Restaurant receives order, accepts it, prepares it, and marks it ready.

3. Rider delivers
- Rider accepts the delivery task, follows the route, and marks delivery complete.

4. Admin supervises
- Admin monitors the full process, service quality, and business performance.

This creates a complete operating chain:
User -> Restaurant -> Rider -> Admin Oversight.

## 5. User Guide (Customer)

### Step 1: Browse Restaurants
Open: https://www.chopnow.co.uk/restaurants

Use these controls:
- Search bar: find restaurant or cuisine
- Filters: narrow options (for example by rating or delivery time)
- Sort options: change how results are listed
- Restaurant card: open full menu

### Step 2: Add Food to Cart
Open a restaurant and click:
- Add to Cart: adds the selected item

Useful actions:
- Choose category to view specific menu sections
- Open cart when ready

### Step 3: Review Cart
Open: https://www.chopnow.co.uk/cart

Buttons and actions:
- Plus/Minus: increase or reduce quantity
- Bin icon: remove an item
- Clear Cart: remove all items
- Apply: apply promo code
- Proceed to Checkout: continue to payment

### Step 4: Checkout and Payment
Open: https://www.chopnow.co.uk/checkout

Required details:
- Name
- Phone number
- Delivery address

Payment options:
- Cash on Delivery
- Card Payment

After successful order placement:
- Customer is redirected to profile orders section.

### Step 5: Track Profile and Orders
Open: https://www.chopnow.co.uk/profile

Tabs available:
- Profile: personal information
- Addresses: saved delivery addresses
- Orders: order history

## 10. Real-Life Operating Scenarios

### Scenario A: Busy Evening Service
- Customers place many orders in a short period.
- Restaurants prioritize new orders and mark ready quickly.
- Riders stay online to maintain fast turnaround.
- Admin monitors bottlenecks and service levels.

### Scenario B: New Restaurant Launch
- Restaurant signs up and completes settings.
- Restaurant builds menu before going live.
- First customer orders are processed and delivered.
- Admin tracks activation and early performance.

### Scenario C: Delivery Capacity Issue
- Fewer riders are available in a location.
- Restaurant experiences slower pickup times.
- Admin monitors rider availability and acts accordingly.

### Scenario D: Customer Service Recovery
- Customer raises an order concern.
- Admin reviews order and account details.
- Operations team resolves issue and follows up.
